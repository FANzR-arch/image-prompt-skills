# 4 套即用预设

每套都符合编译契约。使用时替换标题、主题或室内参考图说明。

## 预设 1 · 文章封面

```text
STYLE ANCHOR — Bauhaus graphic design as a modern design-school method: function-first layout, clear geometry, disciplined typography and no ornament.
A vertical 4:5 editorial article cover for "<LATIN>" and 「<中文标题>」.
LOCKED — every element has a structural or practical role; geometric forms organize the page rather than decorate it; composition is rational, asymmetric, clear and disciplined.
DOMINANT VISUAL — one large circle as visual anchor, one rectangle as title area, one diagonal line as movement, and modular color blocks organizing the page.
MATERIAL & COLOR — flat off-white field, black typography, controlled red and blue structural accents, hard edges, clean print-like surface.
TYPOGRAPHY / TEXT — bold modernist sans-serif typography with clear hierarchy; render exactly "<LATIN>" as the dominant visual title and 「<中文标题>」 as a smaller supporting line; no long body text.
AVOID — generic red-yellow-blue decoration, random geometric stickers, vintage grunge paper, gradients, glossy 3D, fake dates, fake captions, furniture, architecture, Swiss-style excessive neutrality, Constructivist propaganda mood.
QUALITY — clean modernist design-school feeling, sharp geometry, flat color, useful structure, visually clear and immediately usable.
```

## 预设 3 · 栏目 / 活动海报

```text
STYLE ANCHOR — Bauhaus exhibition poster language: type as structure, strict geometry, flat color, experimental but disciplined composition.
A vertical 2:3 exhibition poster for "<POSTER TITLE>".
LOCKED — every element has a structural or practical role; geometric forms organize the poster rather than decorate it; composition is rational, asymmetric, bold and disciplined.
DOMINANT VISUAL — bold sans-serif title integrated with geometric forms, strong asymmetry, large flat planes and exhibition-poster tension.

TYPOGRAPHY / TEXT — render exactly "<POSTER TITLE>" as the main title; use one modernist sans-serif family, clear scale contrast, no random secondary text.

QUALITY — clean modernist design-school feeling, sharp geometry, flat color, useful structure, visually clear and immediately usable.
```

## 预设 4 · 室内照片重绘

```text
STYLE ANCHOR — Bauhaus interior design as a modern design-school method: function, material honesty, modular furniture, clear circulation and disciplined color.
Use the uploaded interior photo as the base image and match its camera perspective and aspect ratio.
LOCKED — preserve the original room geometry, walls, ceiling height, doors, windows, floor plan, camera angle and main furniture positions.
DOMINANT VISUAL — white plaster walls, modular shelving, simple work tables, tubular steel chairs, functional pendant lamps, open circulation and controlled color accents.
MATERIAL & COLOR — warm off-white, black steel, natural wood, leather or canvas, with small controlled red, yellow or blue accents on wall panels or functional objects.
TYPOGRAPHY / TEXT — no fake posters, no unreadable labels, no decorative typography on the walls.
PHOTO REDESIGN RULE — redesign materials, furniture, lighting and color while preserving the original spatial structure and practical use of the room.
AVOID — Scandinavian cozy decor, luxury marble, cyberpunk lighting, boho textiles, random primary-color stickers, unrelated room layout, fake posters with unreadable text, excessive clutter.
QUALITY — bright, rational, useful, design-school-like interior, clear modernist structure, realistic material lighting.
```
